About

I am lebestnoob! The name is self-explanatory... Please forgive my crimes against web development.

I began my journey with Windify as a way to fool classmates into believing I had merged Windows 10 and ChromeOS (rip). At the time, I created shortcuts to a website with favicons set to Windows icons, but wanting to sell the effect better, I decided I was going to recreate the classic start menu. This eventually became a recreation of the Windows 10 settings app, and where I eventually learned how to write mediocre CSS and JavaScript.
